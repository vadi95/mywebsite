About Me!

Hello, I'm 'vadi'. I am currently pursuing my B.Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering at National Institute Of Technology Karnataka. Two things I love the most are coding and DotA. I was introduced to programming in the 6th grade when I learnt Logo and QBasic, since then I have encountered a lot of languages, tools and frameworks that I have absolutely loved working with and still there is a long way to go. In my spare time I usually play table-tennis or am on my laptop playing DotA or watching some movie or a TV show. I got hooked to competitive programming in my second semester, I enjoyed getting by solving tough problems that required some crazy idea which could be implemented efficiently only with an equally crazy data structure.
