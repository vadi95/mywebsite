About Me!

Hello, i'm Vadi. I am currently pursuing my B.Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering at National Institute Of Technology Karnataka. Two things i love the most are coding and DotA. I was introduced to programming in the 6th grade when i learnt Logo and QBasic, since then i have encountered a lot of languages, tools and frameworks and still there is a long way to go. I'm not very good at outdoor sports, but i can play table-tennis and DotA quite well :D. I got hooked to competitive programming from my second semester, i enjoyed getting by solving tough problems that required some crazy idea which could be implemented efficiently only with an equally crazy data structure.
